Download Instructions:
  1. Click on the lesson link to download the lesson as a .zip file.
  2. Unzip the file (if your computer doesn't support this, try 7zip).
  3. Open the README.txt file to read lesson instructions and explanation.
  4. Open the remaining files in your web/text editor and code away!

When working with the adaptive world of web design and technology, different educational methods have to adapt with it. At OpenWeb, we created OpenCode, a collection and community of downloadable lessons for those who learn by trial and error. Taking an approach to education is very difficult, and there isn't just one true way of going about it. Learning, understanding, and applying should be easy for everyone involved. For those who are visual learners, those who understand watching, we have over 100+ tutorials and videos at your request. For those who learn by experience, determination, and trial and error, OpenCode is for you.
