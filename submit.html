SUBMIT

Submit and suggest content to see change in OpenWeb.

Similar to the web itself, OpenWeb relies on user content and suggestions in order to cater content to your interests. The internet is adaptable to an ever changing world and so is OpenWeb. We have created two different forms in order to hear your content effectively and even allow you to submit content for OpenWeb to sponsor, host, and publish on your behalf. Designing the web is a community effort, let's build it together.

Use the request form to send us a message, ask a question, or request a new video and/or tutorial.

Use the submit form to upload your own content or lessons for OpenWeb to host and publish crediting you.
