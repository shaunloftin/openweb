Why study "content"?

Any website can have an excellent back-end and front-end structure utilizing a wide variety of languages. However, without content and defining a clear purpose for your site, it is still lacking the key to become a mastered website.

Videos with the Content tag focus on how to design the front of your page, and how to make it appealing to the eye while maintaining a common "message". All websites have a core message, a purpose, a desire, a solution. Studying and learning about page content and how to find, decide, and target this message in order to maximize potential viewership.

Learn about page content with OpenWeb.
