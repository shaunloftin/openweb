Why learn DESIGN?

Any website could have a powerful front-end and back-end development but without considering the principles of website design, it won't connect with the user.

Tutorials on OpenWeb with the Design Tag are videos and instructions that focus in on small and fundamental things to consider when making your site. By following these instructions and advice from experienced web designers, you can ensure that your website reaches its target audience both quickly and effectively. Many of these pricinples on the focus of color, icons, ans page margins, skills every graphic designer should master.

Learn more about the principles of graphic design in web applications with OpenWeb.
