What are tags?

Tags are OpenWeb's primary way of dividing tutorials. There are sorted by topics covered in the video.


What tags can I choose from?


HTML

Videos under the HTML tag focus on the powerful programming language used to build the structure of any webpage while maintaining proper HTML5 specifications.



CSS

Videos under the CSS tag focus on the programming language used to format, style, and design your website from top to bottom.



Design

Videos under the design tag focus on the layout of your website and elements that make an efficient strucutre for both the designer and user.



Javacsript

Videos under the Javascript tag focus on educating web designers on the simple and interactive programming language used all across the web.



Content

Videos under the content tag focus on the content of your website and how to effectively cater it to your audience and usership.
